Given the information, I would avoid making the website feel like a typical "property sales" website. Since IGA Global doesn't have property listings, the website should position them as a **premium real estate investment and advisory company** rather than a marketplace.

The website should communicate three things within the first few seconds:

* We help people build wealth through Nigerian real estate.
* We are trusted advisors, not just marketers.
* We create opportunities for investors and future homeowners.

The design direction should feel closer to a wealth management firm than a real estate classifieds website.

# Primary Calls to Action

The site should consistently reinforce a single conversion goal, with supporting actions where appropriate:

* **Primary CTA:** Schedule a Consultation
* **Secondary CTA:** Speak With an Investment Advisor
* **Supporting CTA:** Contact Us

This positions IGA Global as a trusted advisory firm focused on guiding clients through real estate investment decisions rather than promoting individual property listings, giving the brand a more premium, consultative presence that aligns with modern real estate investment and wealth-building firms.
